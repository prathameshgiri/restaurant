Our Heritage

A Legacy of Authentic Flavors

Born in the heart of Pune in 1998, Spice Garden began as a small family kitchen where every recipe was a closely guarded secret passed down through three generations. Our founder, Chef Rajan Sharma, brought the royal recipes of Rajasthan to the bustling streets of Maharashtra.

Today, with over 25 years of culinary excellence, we continue to serve each dish with the same passion and precision. We source the finest spices directly from farmers across India — from the saffron fields of Kashmir to the pepper gardens of Kerala.
